Music Production

Music Production is a hobby of mine where I create loops and instrumentals, with various instruments (vst's). My usual objective when opening my DAW is to create melodies that are rather abstract but work well together. For more information, click on the 'Audio' tab above.

Programs

The Digital Audio Workstations (DAWs) that I am familiar with and are Logic Pro, Ableton Live and FL Studio. I currently use Logic Pro X for all of my projects which is where I practice audio engineering and produce my tracks.
